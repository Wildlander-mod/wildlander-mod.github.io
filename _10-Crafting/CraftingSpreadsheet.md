## Complete Crafting Reference

This comprehensive spreadsheet contains all crafting recipes across all toolkits in Wildlander. Use the **Views** dropdown at the top right to filter recipes by:

- **Alchemy** - Potion brewing recipes
- **Breakdown** - Item disassembly recipes
- **Blacksmithing** - Metal armor and weapon crafting
- **Cooking** - Food preparation recipes
- **Enchanting** - Spell imbuing recipes
- **Jewelry** - Gemstone and metal jewelry crafting
- **Raw Materials** - Food and plant harvesting
- **Tanning** - Leather processing and leather armor crafting
- **Sharpening** - Weapon maintenance and repair
