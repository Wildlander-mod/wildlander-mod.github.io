## The Defining Threat

**Vampire Enchantment (25 HP Drain/Absorb per hit)**: Every melee hit he lands heals him for 25 HP while damaging you. If he lands 3 hits per second, he heals 75/s. This is relatively modest compared to other bosses, but it's still a healing mechanic that extends fights.

**Ebony Mail Armor (990 AR + 270 shield)**: Heavy armor provides substantial protection. However, with 550 health, burst damage can still kill him despite the armor.

**Penetrating Strikes Perk (50% lower power attack cost, +5 AP)**: He can power attack frequently without stamina penalty. Each power attack hits harder and penetrates armor slightly (only +5 AP is modest).

**Mace Focus (15 armor penetration, 1.05x damage)**: Modest damage multiplier; not a significant threat multiplier but increases effective DPS slightly.

## Counter Strategies

1. **Burst Damage (Primary Strategy)**:
   - 550 health is low enough that burst damage kills him before healing becomes relevant
   - Focus on maximizing per-hit damage (heavy weapons, enchantments, buffs)
   - His vampire healing only matters if the fight extends; end it quickly
   - Recommended: Two-handed weapons or shock/fire enchanted melee
   - If you deal 80+ DPS, healing is negligible; fight lasts <10 seconds

2. **Outpace Healing via Superior DPS**:
   - If you deal 100 DPS and he heals 75/s via vampirism
   - Net: 25 DPS advantage = 22 seconds to kill (550 Ã· 25)
   - Achievable with solid gear; this is intended difficulty level
   - Focus on consistent sustained damage rather than burst
   - Ranged strategies work here; bow damage (slightly reduced) still exceeds healing

3. **Armor Penetration Build (Optimized)**:
   - His 990 AR (high) can be overwhelmed with 75%+ penetration
   - Penetration perks + enchantments combine multiplicatively
   - Combined with decent base damage = high effective DPS
   - This specialization eliminates the healing problem
   - Viable but requires specific perk investment

4. **Restoration Healing (Attrition Matching)**:
   - If you heal 100/s and he heals 75/s, net advantage is 25/s to you
   - Restoration builds can match or exceed his healing loop
   - This converts fight to a pure attrition match; he cannot win
   - Requires Restoration 100 and multiple healing spells
   - Not the most efficient but guarantees victory through resource war

5. **Elemental Damage (Weakness Exploitation, if any)**:
   - No documented elemental weaknesses on Champion
   - However, Shock/Fire enchantments improve effective DPS regardless
   - Combined with physical damage: base + elemental = higher total DPS
   - Not a primary strategy but reinforces burst approaches

## Critical Mechanics

- **Vampire Enchantment (25 HP absorption per hit)**: Not massive but still relevant to fight duration
- **Ebony Mail Armor (990 AR + shield)**: Significant protection requiring penetration or high base damage
- **Powerful Strike (1.2x power attack damage)**: Power attacks deal 20% bonus damage
- **Stunning Charge (forward power attacks knock down)**: Can knock you down if he closes distance with charging power attacks
- **Penetrating Strikes (50% lower PA cost, +5 AP)**: He can power attack frequently; each penetrates slightly
- **Mace Focus I (1.05x damage, +15 AP)**: Small damage increase (+5% to weapon damage)
- **Knockdown Capable**: His charging power attacks can stun-lock you briefly
- **550 Health (Low for Bosses)**: Lowest health pool among boss encounters; burst damage is very effective
- **Melee-Only**: No ranged or magic threats; fight is pure melee engagement
